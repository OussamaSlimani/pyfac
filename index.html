About us

Pitch Yourself for a Career

Pitch Yourself for a Career" is an annual forum organized by the students of the Computer Science and Applied Mathematics departments at the National Engineering School of Sfax. This event is designed to bring together computer engineering enthusiasts from both academic and industry backgrounds, offering students valuable insights into the professional life of an entrepreneur. Through this forum, participants gain a comprehensive understanding of the challenges and opportunities in the entrepreneurial world, bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application in the field of computer engineering.

Editions

Our previous Editions

For 10 years, we've been empowering connections and creating opportunities at "Pitch Yourself For A Career," and this 10th edition promises to be our most impactful yet.

April 30th, 2016

PYFAC 1

The event featured workshops, guest speakers, and sessions on PFE documentation.

November 19th, 2016

PYFAC 2

Attendees engaged in various workshops, listened to insightful speakers, and explored PFE resources.

November 18th, 2017

PYFAC 3

The event offered a series of workshops, and guidance on PFE documentation.

November 24th, 2018

PYFAC 4

In PYFAC 4, the event included speakers and open discussions, along with workshops focused on starting a business and becoming a social engineer. Additionally, there was a competition and sessions on PFE books.

November 13th, 2019

PYFAC 5

In PYFAC 5, the event featured a variety of workshops, including sessions on body language and CV preparation, followed by an open discussion between students and industry professionals. Afterward, partners delivered speeches, leading into workshops focused on PFE documentation. Concurrently, there were talks on CV writing and freelancing. The event concluded with a roundtable discussion.

November 12-13th, 2020

PYFAC 6

PYFAC 6 was held online and featured a series of speeches, workshops, and discussions on PFE documentation.

November 13-14-20-21th, 2021

PYFAC 7

In pyfac 7, the event featured industry booths, speeches from leading companies, and roundtable discussions. Attendees had the opportunity to participate in multiple workshops, and the event also included the "Smart ENIS" competition organized by paQ.

November 12-19-20th, 2022

PYFAC 8

In PYFAC 8, the event included industry booths and speeches, as well as roundtable discussions. Notable speakers such as Rania Toukebri and Houssem Delai contributed to the program, along with two key conferences and the PFE book workshops.

November 11-12th, 2023

PYFAC 9

In PYFAC 9, the event featured industry booths and speeches, along with roundtable discussions and notable speakers such as Rihab Lajmi and Sami Smaoui. On the second day, there were workshops on PFE books and soft skills, as well as a competition focused on AI in automotive technology.

Who can join us ?

PYFAC is an open forum for all students and professionals, whether from the National Engineering School of Sfax or any other university. It welcomes participants from computer science and other fields, at any level of study. Everyone is welcome to join!
